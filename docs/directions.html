How to Get There

By Plane

  • Take one of several limousine services which operates from the Indianapolis International Airport to Bloomington, Indiana, USA:
    • Carey Indianapolis 1-800-888-4639, (317) 241-7100
    • Classic Touch Limousine 812-339-7269
    • Personal Touch Limousine 812-332-LIMO

  • Bloomington Shuttle Service travels roundtrip from Bloomington to the Indianapolis International Airport seven days a week, nine times a day. For further information, please call 1-800-589-6004, (812) 332-6004.

  • American Trailways also operates daily to Bloomington from the Indianapolis International Airport six times a day. For further information call American Trailways, 812-332-1522.

  • The Indianapolis International Airport is 50-55 minutes north. Travel time is approximately one hour and fifteen minutes because of routinely scheduled bus stops.

By Car-From the North

  • From Indianapolis, Indiana, USA, take Indiana State Road 37 South to the first Bloomington exit (College Avenue). Proceed 3.5 miles to the second stoplight (Budget Rent-A-Car, Denny's restaurant and Holiday Inn on the corners). Turn left onto State Road 45/46 and drive .7 mile to the second stoplight (Indiana State Police Post on the corner); turn right onto Fee Lane.

  • Proceed approximately .5 mile to the next traffic light. As you drive, you will pass some of IU's athletic facilities on your right; the buildings beyond the light are residence halls. Continue on Fee Lane.

  • As you drive up the hill, the IU baseball field is on the left and student residence halls are on your right. Continue on, moving into the right turn lane after you pass under the railway bridge, until you reach the intersection of Fee Lane and Tenth Street.

  • Turn right onto Tenth Street and go to the next traffic light. Turn left onto Woodlawn Avenue and go three blocks. When you reach the intersection of Woodlawn and Seventh Street, the Indiana Memorial Union should be right in front of you. Turn left to access the parking facilities.

By Car-From the East

  • From Columbus, Indiana, USA, take State Road 46 west. You will enter Bloomington's residential and shopping area; State Road 46 becomes East Third Street.

  • At the College Mall shopping Plaza continue straight through the traffic signal on East Third. (The 46 Bypass turns rights at this intersection).

  • Continue on Third Street, and at the sixth traffic light turn right onto Indiana Avenue. On your right you will pass the IU Law School.

  • The first stop sign will be the intersection of Indiana and Kirkwood (5th Street). The Sample Gates will be on your right. Continue on.

  • At the second stop sign turn right onto Seventh Street. Dunn Meadow will be on your right. At the next stop sign, the Indiana Memorial Union will be on your right. Continue on to the parking facilities.

By Car-From the South

  • Take Indiana State Road 37 around Bloomington until you reach the intersection of State Road 37 and State Road 46. Turn right onto the 46 Bypass and continue to the fourth traffic light (Indiana State Police Post on the right). Turn right onto Fee Lane.

  • Proceed approximately .5 mile to the next traffic light. As you drive, you will pass some of IU's athletic facilities on your right; the buildings beyond the light are residence halls. Continue on Fee Lane.

  • As you drive up the hill, the IU baseball field is on the left and student residence halls are on your right. Continue on, moving into the right turn lane after you pass under the railway bridge, until you reach the intersection of Fee Lane and Tenth Street.

By Car-From the West and Southwest

  • Take Indiana State Road 45 or 48 to Indiana State Road 37. Turn left onto State Road 37 and continue north to State Road 46. Turn right onto State Road 46. At the fourth traffic light (Indiana State Police Post on the right) turn right onto Fee Lane.

  • Proceed approximately .5 mile to the next traffic light. As you drive, you will pass some of IU's athletic facilities on your right; the buildings beyond the light are residence halls. Continue on Fee Lane.
