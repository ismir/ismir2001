Parking

Hotel guests of the Indiana Memorial Union may park in the IMU parking lot free of charge. Simply show the parking attendant your room key.

If you are staying at another hotel, you have several options:

  • Use the free shuttle service between the IMU and participating area hotels..

  • Use the parking pass available at the Conference Office to park in the IMU lot for $6.50 per day. Be sure to get there early to find a spot!

  • Park at one of the following parking garages, all within walking distance of the IMU. Rates are $1.25 per half hour with a maximum of $12.50 per day.

    • Poplars Garage  (6th between Grant & Dunn)
    • 10th & Fee Lane Garage  (Corner of 10th Street and Fee Lane)
    • Atwater Garage  (Woodlawn Ave between 3rd St. and Atwater Ave)
    • Jordan Garage  (Jordan Avenue between 3rd Street and 7th Street)

Parking on campus is limited. You're advised to take advantage of the free shuttle service. If you decide to park on campus, be sure to give yourself plenty of time to look for a parking space. Park ONLY in pay lots or garages; if you park in a spot requiring a university sticker, you could be ticketed or towed.
