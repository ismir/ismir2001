Indiana University's Bloomington campus is considered one of the most beautiful in the United States. Located in the heart of Bloomington, the University is a careful blend of traditional and modern architecture. The campus buildings, made of locally quarried limestone, add beauty and local charm to the scenic campus. The University offers the finest in opera, theater, music, and dance.

Situated in the rolling hills of southern Indiana, Bloomington is one of Indiana's most popular travel destinations. It is filled with the warmth and charm of a small town plus the cultural and recreational amenities of a major metropolitan city. Local restaurants include an impressive array of ethnic cuisine, many within a comfortable walking distance of the Indiana Memorial Union.
